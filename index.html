York's Radical Library

Anarcho Cosmic Activist Books, created in April 2021, is York’s new radical library: a grass-roots group sharing knowledge and ideas through books.

See our Instagram page for the most up to date information and list of books.

Joining us

Donate a book, or pay £5 to join! Simple as. You can sign up through our membership form. Make sure we don’t have the book by contacting us before donating.

Donate

We have a PayPal you can make money donations through.

Pop ups

We will be running pop up libraries every Tuesdays and Sundays in York, check out Instagram for updates. Make sure all book requests are made at least the day before you collect them.
